Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AccessMap PH ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described here.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryExamples
Account information Email address, display name, profile photo (if you sign in with Google)
Contributions Accessibility reports, map pins, descriptions you submit
Usage & security data Authentication tokens, sign-in timestamps, rate-limit counters

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display your Contributions to other users on the map
  • To detect and prevent spam, abuse, and duplicate submissions
  • To communicate with you about your account, such as email verification
  • To improve and develop new features for the Service

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on the following processors to operate AccessMap PH:

  • Google Firebase — authentication (email/password and Google sign-in)
  • MongoDB Atlas — storage of location, report, and account data
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — place search and reverse geocoding

These providers process data under their own privacy policies and security practices.

4. Sharing of Information

Accessibility reports and pins you contribute are publicly visible to other users of the Service by design — this is the core purpose of AccessMap PH. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of AccessMap PH and its users.

5. Data Retention

We retain account and Contribution data for as long as your account is active or as needed to operate the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting the maintainers.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Update your display name and photo through your sign-in provider
  • Sign out of your account at any time
  • Choose not to submit reports or pins — browsing the map does not require an account
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal data

Email/password accounts must verify their email address before they can submit reports or create map pins.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or requests can be directed to the AccessMap PH maintainers via the project repository or support email once published.
